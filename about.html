How It All Began

Founded in 2015 by three graduate students from Northeastern Illinois University, who decided to take their skills to the next level. Kaushal Patel, Kakha Urigashvili, and Mohamed Hamadelniel were constantly asked for advice on how to make quality mobile and desktop applications. They formed this company to offer their services at a low cost and help business improve their efficiency and productivity with quality custom applications.

Our Philosophy

Technology is always changing so our philosophy is to change along with it. All our developers are students for life. We only employ developers that are eager to learn, and we provide them with all the tools necessary to enrich themselves. We believe in staying current with the latest technology.
